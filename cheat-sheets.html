CSS

HTML

Layout

head Where your metadata goes, like title
body Where the content of your page goes
header The top section of a page
footer The bottom section of a page
nav The navigation/menu to go from page to page or section to section
section Any section within a page
Should be the sibling of header and footer

Text

p A paragraph
a A link
Include href attribute
ul An unordered list (bullet points)
ol An ordered list (numbered)
li An list item
Use as children of ul or ol

Media

img An image
Include src and alt attribute

Spacing

br A line break (like enter/return key)
hr A horizontal line
